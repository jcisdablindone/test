About Us

CDT Roofing is a family run business which prides itself on providing a professional service at the highest quality. We offer a wide area of expertise and guarantee an excellent finish on all completed work


We cover all aspects of roofing and building work, including the following:

  • Flat Roofing ( Felt )
  • Fiberglass Roofing
  • Boarding
  • Tiling
  • Slating
  • Sheeting
  • Polycarbonate
  • Chimneys
  • Lead Work
  • Gutters / Down Pipes
  • All UPVC Work ( i.e. Fascias, Soffits, Bargeboards)
  • All Aspects of General Building Work ( i.e. Rendering, Plastering, Brickwork, Block Work)
  • All Interior Work ( i.e. Ceilings, Walls, Carpentry, Electrical, Plumbing)
